Strawberry Cheesecake

Description

So creamy and good - the strawberry flavor really comes through in the cheesecake and the chocolate crust and ganache work great with the strawberries. You will Love this!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chopped fresh strawberries
  • 5 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • Â½ cup granulated sugar
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 5 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1 tablespoon freshly squeezed lemon juice
  • 2 drops pink gel food coloring

Steps

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Wrap outside of a lightly greased (with cooking spray) 9-inch springform pan with a double layer of heavy-duty aluminum foil.

  2. Step 2: Stir together cookies, melted butter, and granulated sugar in a bowl. Press onto bottom and 1-inch up sides of prepared pan.

  3. Step 3: Bake in the preheated oven until crust is set, 7 to 8 minutes. Transfer to a wire rack; cool completely, 30 minutes.

  4. Step 4: Preheat the oven again to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).

  5. Step 5: Process chopped strawberries in a food processor until completely smooth, about 1 minute, stopping to scrape down sides as needed. Set strawberry puree aside.

  6. Step 6: Beat cream cheese with a stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachment on medium speed until creamy, 2 to 3 minutes. Gradually add sugar and flour, beating until smooth, about 1 minute. Add whole eggs, 1 at a time, beating on low speed just until blended after each addition (do not overbeat). Add egg yolks, 1 at a time, beating until just blended after each addition.
